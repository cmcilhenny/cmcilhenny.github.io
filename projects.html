TweeterListener

Adventure Library

An awesome, DIY, choose-your-own-adventure site that allows a user to explore other authors' adventures and make your own. The library uses sidekiq workers to link to other libraries and continually update its listings. Technologies utilized: Ruby on Rails, Sidekiq, Redis, APIs/JSON.

beeThree
